history

Princess Sumaya University for Technology was established in 1991 and is the only private, non-profit university in Jordan owned by the Royal Scientific Society (RSS), the oldest center for applied research in Jordan. Princess Sumaya University is part of Al-Hassan Scientific City and is based in Amman. The main mission of Princess Sumaya University is to educate students ...


why Princess Sumaya University?

Being part of El Hassan Science City, Princess Sumaya University for Technology strives to create an entrepreneurship culture among its students. Princess Sumaya University for Technology is licensed and all its programs are accredited by the Jordanian Higher Education Accreditation Commission, and it continues to expand its curricula to cover the requirements of modern education methods and expertise required by the local and international business markets. Princess Sumaya University for Technology strives to be a pioneer in building the ICT and electronics sectors in Jordan and a platform for scientific research for various Jordanian institutions. Our goal is simply for Princess Sumaya University for Technology to be the "University of the Future" where highly talented and qualified students are educated and ready to join the competing markets of the 21st century and beyond. We at Princess Sumaya University for Technology seek to advance excellence, scholarship, commitment, innovation and entrepreneurial spirit that will drive development in the private sector.
